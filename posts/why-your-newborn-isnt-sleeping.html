Why Your Newborn Isn’t Sleeping (And What You Can Do Tonight)

2026-02-15

It’s the middle of the night. You’ve fed your baby. Changed the diaper. Rocked. Shushed. Repeated. And somehow... they’re awake again. If you’re wondering whether you’re doing something wrong — you’re not. Newborn sleep feels chaotic because it is chaotic. Not broken. Not spoiled. Just biologically new.

What’s Normal

Newborns wake often. Their stomachs are tiny. Their sleep cycles are short. Waking every 2–3 hours is normal.

What You Can Do Tonight

Put baby down a little earlier. Keep nights dark and quiet. Use swaddling (if safe) and white noise. Pause briefly before picking up at every stir.

Reset Expectations

Sleeping through the night is not a newborn milestone. You don’t need perfection. You need progress.
